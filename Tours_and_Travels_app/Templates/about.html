Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Superior Tours and Travels began as a small team of passionate travelers with a simple mission: to share the world's most incredible places with like-minded adventurers. What started as a modest operation organizing local hiking trips has grown into a premier global travel company with operations in over 30 countries.

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, started the company after her transformative backpacking journey across Southeast Asia. She realized that travel has the power to change lives, broaden perspectives, and create connections across cultures. Today, we're proud to have helped over 50,000 travelers discover the magic of exploration.

We're not just tour operators - we're storytellers, experience curators, and your personal guides to the world's wonders. Our team of travel experts has collectively visited over 100 countries, and we use that firsthand knowledge to craft journeys that go beyond the typical tourist experience.
