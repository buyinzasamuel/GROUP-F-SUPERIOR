Overview

Experience the magic of Bali with our 7-day adventure tour that takes you through the island's most breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and spiritual heart. From the lush rice terraces of Ubud to the stunning beaches of Uluwatu, this tour offers a perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and cultural immersion. Enjoy private tours, luxury accommodations, and authentic Balinese experiences that will leave you with unforgettable memories.

Tour Highlights

Beach Paradise

Visit Bali's most beautiful beaches including Nusa Dua, Padang Padang, and Jimbaran Bay.

Waterfall Exploration

Discover hidden waterfalls like Tegenungan and Sekumpul with private guided tours.

Culinary Delights

Enjoy authentic Balinese cuisine with cooking classes and gourmet dining experiences.

Wellness & Relaxation

Rejuvenate with traditional Balinese spa treatments and yoga sessions.

Itinerary

1

Arrival in Bali - Ubud Cultural Tour

Upon arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport, you'll be transferred to your luxury resort in Ubud. After settling in, embark on a cultural tour visiting the Sacred Monkey Forest, Ubud Palace, and local artisan markets. Enjoy a welcome dinner with traditional Balinese dance performance.

2

Tegalalang Rice Terraces & Waterfall Adventure

Start your day with sunrise yoga session followed by breakfast overlooking the jungle. Visit the stunning Tegalalang Rice Terraces and learn about Balinese farming traditions. In the afternoon, trek to Tegenungan Waterfall for a refreshing swim. Evening at leisure to explore Ubud.

3

Mount Batur Sunrise Trek & Hot Springs

Early morning trek to Mount Batur summit to witness breathtaking sunrise over Bali. Enjoy breakfast with volcano views before descending to relax at Toya Devasya Hot Springs. Return to Ubud for afternoon spa treatment and leisure time.

4

Transfer to Uluwatu - Beach Day & Sunset Cliff Dinner

Travel to Uluwatu with stops at Tanah Lot Temple and Balangan Beach. Check into your beachfront villa and spend the afternoon relaxing or surfing. Enjoy a romantic cliffside dinner at Single Fin with spectacular ocean views.
